Tips for improvement

Here we will look at how to improve your data visualization.

Accuracy and clarity are essential to effective visualization, as well as memorability, persuasion, and motivation to action.

When designing your graphics, keep clarity and comprehension in mind and experiment with different chart types that might help your reader understand your story and its key elements.
